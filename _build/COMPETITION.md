# Competition — Landscape map

> Last updated: 2026-04-18
> Scope: direct and adjacent players in SaaS for live performing arts management

## 1. Direct competitor — Ares (ares-app.com)

### Company
- **Operator**: Bresca Digital SCCL
- **Location**: Mollerussa, Lleida (town of ~15k inhabitants)
- **Type**: Catalan cooperative (SCCL), founded 2023
- **Core business**: digital consulting, Odoo installs, web design, custom programming, Kit Digital subsidy management, training
- **Ares is a side product**, not their main line. No dedicated full-time product team.
- Funded via EU Next Generation + INAEM grant (development subsidy, not revenue)

### Pricing (as seen in public tariff page)
| Plan | Price | Admins | Storage | Key features |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| LITE | 30€/mes | 1 | 100 MB | bolos, workers, materials, CRM básico, multilingual docs |
| GENERAL | 60€/mes | unlimited | 1 GB | + calendar conflict detection, prenòmines, Seg. Social, stats, processos automàtics |
| Setup fee | 150€ one-time | | | installation, initial training, customization |
| Annual discount | -1 month | | | 12 months for the price of 11 |

**Paid add-ons**:
- Telegram availability requests: 10€/mes
- Google Calendar sync: 15€/mes ← this alone is a red flag. In 2026, every competitor bundles this.
- Template customization: 25€ per template
- Bulk data load: 50€ one-time
- 2h online training session: 50€

### Features — what Ares does
- Gig/event management (bolos, funcions, residències)
- Worker + material + vehicle resource management (unlimited)
- Availability confirmation
- Calendar conflict detection (only in GENERAL)
- Automatic pre-payroll calculation (prenòmines)
- Seguridad Social altas/bajas report generation
- Subsidy-oriented statistics
- Communications to contact groups
- Automated data import from predefined templates
- Admin documentation / contract management
- Multilingual documents
- Basic CRM — client directory, not sales pipeline
- Document personalization with company identity

### Features — what Ares does NOT do
- **Difusión / booking outreach / distribution CRM**
- Real sales pipeline with stages (propuesta → confirmado → contratado → ejecutado → cobrado)
- Tech rider (stage plots, light plots, sound patches, technical inventory)
- Tour logistics (routes, accommodation, transport, per-diems)
- Technical integrations (Qlab, Ableton, EOS, Figure 53)
- Multi-country support beyond Spanish labor compliance
- Serious media storage (the 100MB–1GB caps are a joke for an archive-of-record tool)
- Agile product iteration (side product cadence)

### Positioning summary
**Internal operations + Spanish labor compliance**, for small-to-medium Spanish companies that need to get their HR paperwork and bolos scheduling in order. Not for companies that need distribution, touring, or technical production management.

### Traction signals
- Presented at Mostra Igualada (Catalan kids/youth theatre market)
- At least one known client in Catalonia (per Marco's anecdotal knowledge)
- **No public customer testimonials on their site after ~2 years operating** → weak traction signal
- No news coverage of Ares launch or growth
- No LinkedIn company growth data on Bresca Digital

## 4. Strategic takeaways

1. **Ares is weaker competitor than initially assumed.** Side product of a small cooperative with no public traction signals, subsidized, not a full-time product team. Ventana competitiva más ancha de lo previsto.
2. **Spanish labor compliance is NOT our battle.** Regulatory trap. Integrate, don't replicate. Users who need both use Ares + Hour.
3. **Hour's moat candidates (what Ares cannot easily copy)**:
   - Multi-country i18n from day one
   - Serious media storage (R2 integration)
   - Technical integrations (Qlab/Ableton/EOS) — requires insider knowledge
   - Iteration velocity with Claude Code
   - Insider product design (Marco = target user)
4. **Watch signal**: when Bresca's EU grant cycle ends or doesn't renew, Ares pricing or existence may change. At that point their existing clients become Hour leads.
5. **Pricing stance**: enter at parity with Ares (25–60€) then go above with PRO tier (120€) where Hour delivers features Ares does not offer.
